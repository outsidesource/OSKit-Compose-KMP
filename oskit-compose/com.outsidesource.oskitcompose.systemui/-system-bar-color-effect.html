SystemBarColorEffect Configures the system toolbars for Android and iOS. On desktop this effect is a no-op.

iOS Notes: In order to use SystemBarColorEffect in iOS, replace ComposeUIViewController with OSComposeUIViewController and use ignoresSafeArea(.all) on your primary View. navigationBarIconColor is a no-op in iOS.
